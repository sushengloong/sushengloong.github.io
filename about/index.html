About

Ohai, I am Su Sheng Loong a.k.a. SSL. I work as a consultant of Infocomm Development Authority (IDA) Singapore. However, I am deployed to Ministry of Manpower (MOM) Singapore to develop mission-critical government e-services.

CodeSSL.com is my personal blog where I write about programming, software engineering and some other tech stuff. The text and opinions are all mine. I use Jekyll whereby the blog design and content are edited completely in Vim. The source code can be found at here and the entire blog is hosted on GitHub Pages.

My areas of expertise are Ruby on Rails, Ansible, Agile, Extreme Programming (XP), DevOps and Continuous Delivery (CD). I always try to teach myself new technology like ReactJS, Node.JS, Swift on iOS, Docker and some functional programming languages.

When I am not programming, I like to dance, play sports and take photographs with my modest Olympus E-M10.
