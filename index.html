About Su Sheng Loong

Ohai, I am Su Sheng Loong (code name “SSL”). I work as a full-stack software engineer at Salesforce.com.

me visiting Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco during a Salesforce.com business trip

Prior to joining Salesforce.com, I was a software developer of Government Technology Agency of Singapore (GovTech) and Ministry of Manpower (MOM) Singapore.

with minister tan chuan-jin at mom hackathon

My main area of expertise is agile web application development. The bulk of my work involves Scala+Play, Python+Django and CoffeeScript+Angular.JS. I am also proficient in Ruby on Rails and Java+Spring Boot.

At work, I practise Test-driven Development (TDD), Continuous Integration (CI), pair-programming and refactoring. I care a lot about writing not only functional but also efficient, maintainable and secure code.
