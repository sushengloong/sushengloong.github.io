About Su Sheng Loong

Ohai, I am Su Sheng Loong a.k.a. SSL. I work as a consultant of Infocomm Development Authority (IDA) Singapore. Instead of working at IDA headquarter, I am deployed to Ministry of Manpower (MOM) Singapore to develop mission-critical government e-services.

With Minister Tan Chuan-jin at MOM Hackathon

My main area of expertise is agile web development with Ruby on Rails. At work, I practise Test-driven Development (TDD), Continuous Integration (CI), pair-programming and refactoring. I care a lot about writing not only functional but also efficient, maintainable and secure code.

Speaker at Red Dot Ruby Conference
With Matz, the creator of Ruby programming language

In addition, I am also involved in DevOps activities such as developing Bash/Ansible scripts and setting up build/deployment pipelines. The automation in turn enables Continuous Delivery (CD) for our project.

When I am not programming, I like to dance, travel and take photographs with my modest Olympus OM-D E-M10.
